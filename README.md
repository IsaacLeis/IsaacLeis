<h2>👨‍🔬 About me</h2>

<p>
I am a PhD candidate focused on <b>bioprocess modelling, scale-up, and optimization of biorefinery systems.</b>  
My work combines mathematical modelling of unit operations with system-level integration to evaluate process performance through techno-economic assessment and identify strategies to improve economic viability.
</p>

<p>My main interests include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Bioprocess modelling and simulation</li>
  <li>Techno-economic assessment (TEA)</li>
  <li>Process scale-up and design</li>
  <li>Developing computational tools for process engineering</li>
</ul>
